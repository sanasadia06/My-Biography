About Sana

Sana Sadia is a rising literary star from Pakistan who began her writing journey at the young age of 17. Born with a passion for storytelling, she balances her scientific academic background with creative expression.

Her debut work was published on September 20, 2024, marking the beginning of what promises to be a remarkable writing career. At just 18 years old, Sana has already completed her Intermediate in Science while nurturing her talent for prose.

Based in Pakistan, Sana draws inspiration from her cultural heritage, contemporary society, and the universal human experience. Her writing reflects the unique perspective of a young Pakistani woman navigating tradition and modernity.
